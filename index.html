Treaty 1

What is treaty 1?

Treaties are agreements made between the Canadian government and the indigenous people that are living there about their land, and rights. Treaty 1 is one of the first treaties that was made and signed in 1871.

Treaty 1 is signed in lower fort Garry, also known as the stone fort, located near the town of Selkirk, north-west of Winnipeg. The indeginous tribes that participated were the Anishinaabe, and the swampy Cree.
