Treaty 1

What is treaty 1?

Treaties are agreements made between the Canadian government and the indigenous people who are already living on the land. The agreements were usually about sharing their land, and their rights.

Treaty 1 is one of the first treaties that was negotiated and signed in 1871. The area of treaty 1 covered most of southern Manitoba, including cities like Winnipeg and Brandon.

Where was treaty 1 signed?

The treaty was signed in lower fort Garry, also known as the stone fort. It is located near the town of Selkirk, north-east of Winnipeg. The Canadian government chose lower fort garry because it was a good meeting place.
